About Cozmo

Cozmo has been practising for over 13 years, building a quiet reputation almost entirely through word of mouth. People come back, and they send their friends.

She trained directly with Louise Mita, the founder of Integrative Quantum Medicine, completing all three levels in eleven months — a process that ordinarily takes five years. She then became the first IQM teacher in Europe.
